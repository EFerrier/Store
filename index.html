Welcome to the Annuity Store.

Our goal is to provide a platform that provides you a way to compare and purchase the best annuity for you.

What is an Annuity?

An annuity is an investment in your future. An annuity involves two parties, for our explanation Party A and Party B. Party A pays Party B a lump sum and then Party B makes payments over X years to Party A. The reason Party A does this is because they earn Y% interest on the lump sum over the X years of the annuity.

Why would someone sell me an Annuity?

Financial services companies sell annuities because they believe they can invest the lump sum and earn a better return on the lump sum than the interest rate they are paying out.
